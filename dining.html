Our dining hall was built in 1308 by a king who loved gourmet dining so much, he constructed a standalone building to house his kitchen and dining facility. Our menu features farm-to-table options with ingredients sources from local farms and fisheries. Whether you're vegetarian, gluten-free, or lactose-intolerant, there are many delicious options that will leave you licking your fingers and ready to take on another day of sight-seeing and adventuring.
